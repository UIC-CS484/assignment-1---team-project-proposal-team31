Audience Section

Who am I serving?

  • Spotify users
  • Dancers
  • Fans of music and dance
  • People interested in weather data or just curious about music

Goals

  • To promote the enjoyment of music and dance
  • To promote getting outdoors
  • To promote being active

Why?

I enjoy listening to music and I wish I could spend some more time outside doing so, so I figured that pairing music and weather data would be fun.

Some songs remind me of certain times of day or seasons. It would be cool to input the name of a song, and use some of its attributes to match it up with the current climate.
